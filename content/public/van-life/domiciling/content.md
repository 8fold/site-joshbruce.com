# Domiciling

{!! dateblock !!}

You are only domiciled in one state a time. just because you live in a state, doesnt mean you are domiciled or a resident of that state.

Things to comsider:

1. How much does it cost to live that state?
2. How hard is it to become a resident?
3. State politics?
4. Located where you spend the most time? 

Must go to that state for driver’s license. With Real ID, can be renewed every 8 years. (Not sure if this is the case in every state.
